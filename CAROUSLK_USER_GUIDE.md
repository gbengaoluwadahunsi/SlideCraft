# 🎨 Carouslk User Guide

## What is Carouslk?

**Carouslk** is an AI-powered tool that helps you create beautiful, scroll-stopping carousels for LinkedIn, Instagram, X (Twitter), newsletters, and pitch decks—in just minutes, without any design skills!

Simply type in your idea, and let AI transform it into a polished, multi-slide carousel ready to publish.

🌐 **Website:** [carouslk.com](https://www.carouslk.com)

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Create an Account
1. Go to [carouslk.com](https://www.carouslk.com)
2. Click **"Start Creating"** or **"Get Started"**
3. Sign up with your email or Google account
4. Verify your email (check your inbox!)

### Step 2: Access the Dashboard
Once logged in, you'll land on the **Dashboard**—your creative workspace where all the magic happens.

## 🎯 Creating Your First Carousel

### Option A: Use AI to Generate Content

1. Click the **✨ AI Generate** button in the sidebar
2. Enter your topic or paste your content
   - Example: *"5 tips for better productivity"*
   - Or paste an article/blog post you want to convert
3. Choose your settings:
   - **Number of slides** (3-50)
   - **Writing style** (Professional, Casual, Bold, etc.)
   - **Words per slide** (optional)
4. Click **Generate** and watch the AI create your carousel!

### Option B: Start from Scratch

1. Click **+ Add Slide** to create new slides
2. Click on any text to edit it directly
3. Use the sidebar to:
   - Change colors
   - Pick a theme
   - Add images or videos
   - Customize fonts

## ✏️ Editing Your Carousel

### Basic Editing
- **Click any text** to edit it inline
- **Drag slides** in the left panel to reorder them
- **Click the emoji** area to add/change emojis on slides

### Styling Options

| Feature | How to Access |
|---------|---------------|
| **Change Theme** | Click the 🎨 palette icon in the sidebar |
| **Custom Colors** | Settings panel → Background, Text, Accent colors |
| **Fonts** | Settings panel → Font Family dropdown |
| **Text Size** | Settings panel → Font Scale slider |
| **Text Alignment** | Choose Left, Center, or Right |

### Adding Media
- **Images:** Click the 🖼️ image icon → Upload or paste URL
- **Videos:** Paste a YouTube link or upload a video file
- **Charts:** Create data visualizations (bar, line, or pie charts)

### Pro Tips for Editing
- Use **bold** for key points (highlight text → click B)
- Use *highlights* for important terms (highlight text → click I)
- Keep your title punchy—3-6 words works best
- Add a strong hook on your cover slide

## 💡 Tips for Creating Great Carousels

### Content Tips
1. **Start with a hook** — Your first slide should grab attention
2. **One idea per slide** — Don't overcrowd
3. **Use numbers** — "5 Tips..." performs better than "Some Tips..."
4. **End with a CTA** — Tell people what to do next (follow, comment, share)

### Design Tips
1. **Less is more** — White space is your friend
2. **Consistent colors** — Stick to 2-3 colors max
3. **Large text** — People scroll fast, make it readable
4. **Add visuals** — Emojis, icons, or images break up text

### Performance Tips
1. **10-15 slides** is the sweet spot for engagement
2. **Cover slide** should make people want to swipe
3. **Include your handle** so people know who made it
4. **Test different themes** to see what resonates

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I use Carouslk on mobile?**  
A: Yes! The website works on mobile browsers, though the desktop experience is better for editing.

**Q: What if I run out of exports?**  
A: Free users get 5 exports total. Upgrade to Starter or Pro for unlimited exports.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Yes, anytime. Go to Dashboard → Billing → Cancel. Your projects remain accessible.

**Q: Do my carousels expire?**  
A: No! Your projects are saved forever, even on the free plan.

**Q: Can I import my own documents?**  
A: Yes! You can upload Word documents (.docx) and the AI will extract the content.
